ISLAND KAYAKING

Train for the Herron Island Kayak Race, or just have fun! Meet at the North Beach Monday, Wednesday, and Friday evenings at 5:30 p.m.! Bring all your gear, including a towel, sunscreen, water or wine, and a hat! The towel can be used as a backrest or to dry off in your vehicle.
