HIRING HELP ON THE ISLAND


Hiring help or services on Herron Island can be difficult. Many (most) contractors and service providers don't want to come to the island. The businesses below have paid to be listed on this page, and all come to the island.

 As you can guess, here is where we say all that important legal stuff. HMC makes no representations as to the quality of the work they do. HMC does not check to see if they're licensed or bonded when they say they are, or even whether they're a legitimate business. HMC doesn't guarantee their work, or even recommend their services.

How to decide whom to hire? Our recommendation is to call your neighbors and friends on Herron Island. What contractor have you used? How much did you pay? Did you have problems? You can also check with the state to determine licensing and bonding. You can check online to confirm Contactor's License & Bonding Information.
