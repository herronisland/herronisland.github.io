LARGE VEHICLE RESTRICTIONS

Herron Island has so many visitors on the holiday weekends of Memorial Day, July 4th and Labor Day that vehicles over 25 feet in length, or any combination of vehicle with trailer over 25 feet, are not transported on the ferry after the noon run on the Thursday before the holiday weekend.
