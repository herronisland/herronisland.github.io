JULY FOURTH PARADE

Join the July 4th Parade on Herron Island. Annual Event! Starts at Goodpastor Park at 12:30 on July 2nd - Sunday. We parade down Maple Blvd. and end at the North Beach Park for popsicles. All entries are welcome!! Join the fun with all your island friends and family. You can decorate a car, bus, bike,wagon,cart, or buggy. Get creative! You can decorate yourself and walk or decorate the kids and run. This will be the best celebration ever so join in and show your spirit.
