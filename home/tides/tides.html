TIDE CHARTS
  • Ferry delays are likely at tide levels below -1.0 foot.

  • Tides 14.5 feet and over may result in flooding of the North Beach area and the mainland parking area. During times of heavy rains and wind the chances increase.

  • Software used does not change to Daylight Saving Time until the first weekend in April and changes back to Standard Time the fourth weekend in October.  Adjustments must be made for current law, in which Daylight Time begins the first weekend in March and ends the first weekend in November.
