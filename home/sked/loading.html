VEHICLE
LOADING - UNLOADING INSTRUCTIONS

Speed Limit on Ramp 5 mph

1. When light turns green, proceed across Cement Pier to wood ramp.
2. One vehicle at a time on wood ramp.
3. Stop at bottom of wood ramp until a crewmember directs your vehicle
into a lane on the ferry deck.
4. Set your parking brake. Shut off motor.
5. Remain in your vehicle until ramp gate is closed.
6. Follow crews instructions for loading and unloading the ferry at all times.
7. When unloading Do Not START motor until directed by crew member
and then release parking brake and unload from ferry as directed by crew.
Thanks for your cooperation

NO SMOKING & /or DRUGS or ALCOHOL consumption
on FERRY at ANYTIME!
