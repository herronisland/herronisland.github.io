Special Membership Meeting



A special membership meeting will be held at noon on Saturday, March 10th to vote on approval of two expenditure issues. The first proposition seeks membership approval to spend up to $15,000 for an application for long-term USDA financing to upgrade the island water system, while the second proposition seeks approval to spend up to $29,000 for resurfacing the island roads. Ballots have been mailed to the address of record of each member and may be returned by mail or submitted in person at the meeting. Detailed explanations of the propositions are included with the ballot.
