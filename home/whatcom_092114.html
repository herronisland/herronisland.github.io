From Claudia Ellsworth



     



Vigor Marine had their two favorite small ferries in at the same time. The Whatcom Chief, which runs from Bellingham to Lummi island, was just leaving as the Charlie Wells arrived. The Chief is 93 feet long, has 38 runs a day, and carried 171,000 passengers last year, more than double what the CW does. It is owned and operated by Whatcom County.
