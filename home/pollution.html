AIR QUALITY BURN BAN CANCELLED

Dec. 4, 2002 - The Stage One Burn Ban issued on Wednesday, Nov. 27, for King, Kitsap, Pierce and Snohomish counties has been cancelled.

The inversion that has been in place since last week has lifted enough to allow much of the trapped pollution to clear out and return air quality to moderate in most areas. However, relatively stagnant conditions will return on Thursday and Friday. If those conditions persist through the weekend, the burn ban may need to be reinstated.

For details on air quality burn bans, check the Puget Sound Clean Air Agency  web site or the toll-free information line at 1-800-595-4341.
