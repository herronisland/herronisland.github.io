HOW TO USE THE PRINTABLE GUEST PASS:

  1. Print this page on your printer.
  2. Cut out the pass (don't give the crew the full page.)
  3. Fill in the guest's name.
  4. The member's name must be printed, and the member must sign the pass.
  5. Fill in the date(s) the pass will be valid.
  6. Check the payment method.  If paid by member, attach payment to pass.  Charges are only allowed for contractors and commercial vehicles.
