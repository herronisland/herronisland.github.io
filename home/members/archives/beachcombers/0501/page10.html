CANDIDATE: Janet Podell

My name is Janet Podell and I have been a member of the Herron Island community for two years. I hope to build a cabin on my small lot at the south end of the island and eventually retire there. Over these past two years I have really come to appreciate what a remarkable and magical place Herron Island is, and feel that I can offer some of my personal time to help contribute to its successful operation. For the past year I have been serving on the Finance and Communication committees and through this involvement have experienced a glimpse of the how the island is managed. As a Board Member I would have exposure to the other operational components which keep the island running smoothly and perhaps could utilize some of the business skills I have acquired over the years for the benefit of this community. I believe that Herron Island is a beautiful and unique place that continues to need the stewardship of people who are committed to maintaining is viability.

Experience and Skills

I have worked for The Boeing Company for 14 years as a Buyer and Procurement Manager. I currently manage a group of Buyers who are responsible for the purchase of various supplies and services for Boeing facilities. This computes to about $150+ million per year over which I have direct responsibility. My procurement and business experience actually precedes Boeing by about 13 years. I worked in the Aerospace industry in Southern California during those previous years and bring a total of 27 years of business experience to the table. Additionally, over the course of my career I have managed process improvement teams and developed employee recognition programs. I have strong communication skills and pride myself on being organized. The nature of the work I do also requires me to hone and practice my problem solving skills on a daily basis.

In summary

I believe that a Board member should serve the community with skill, commitment, and a high degree of common sense. I have observed the current and past Boards and have witnessed first hand the talent, skill, and commitment they bring to the table. I believe I possess some of the necessary attributes and would be honored to serve as a Board member.
