No Reservations needed
The Community Center is magically transformed into a European bistro by elves who arrive early in the morning. When you arrive at 6 PM you'll pay for your dinner ticket and receive a bidding number. After a libation or two (BYOB), you proceed to the outdoor BBQ where you choose a T-bone steak, grilled to order, or a nice piece of chicken. From there, it's off to the salad bar for tossed green salad, baked potato with sour cream, and garlic bread. Please note: this is a one time through buffet, not an "all you can eat." Just as you're finishing dessert, about 8 PM, the auction begins. (Don't forget your checkbook or cash. All items must be paid for on the evening of the auction.)

Last year, more than 120 people came for the steak dinner and raised almost $2500 for Boosters projects. The cost is $7.50 per person. No reservations needed, but seating is on a first come - first served basis, and we only have so many chairs. (Late arrivals brought their own chairs last year.)

And don't think that this is "just a small island auction". Last year's items included everything from a $10 haircut to a weekend in the Jacuzzi Suite at the Inn in Gig Harbor. We also had gourmet cooking equipment, hand-made bird feeders with seed, and a collection of extra-large pipe wrenches. You need it?  You'll find it at the Boosters Annual Auction!!
