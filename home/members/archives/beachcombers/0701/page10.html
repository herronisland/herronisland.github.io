WANTED:  Booster Members


How much does it cost? 

$2.50 per person, or $5 per family, per year

When do they meet? 
The Boosters meet at 6 PM on the 2nd Saturday of each summer month, and every other month starting in October. This is the same Saturday as the Board Meeting which is held at 10:00 AM.

When is the next meeting?
Come to the Potluck/Meeting at 6 PM on Saturday, August 11. Bring an entrée, side dish or dessert. After a brief Booster meeting, join your neighbors to share some good food, great conversation and get involved in island projects that benefit us all!

Where do I sign up? 
You can become a Booster member by signing up at the Booster table at any island event, or drop off a check at the office made out to The Boosters, or join at any Boosters Potluck.
