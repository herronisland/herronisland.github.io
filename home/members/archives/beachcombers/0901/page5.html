PREVIEW OF COMING EVENTS:

November 10th: The next Boosters' meeting/potluck is November 10. We're asking everyone who attends to bring chili, chowder, salad or rolls. Also, everyone is asked to bring a Christmas ornament so that we can decorate the ferry and the Community Building.
December 8th:  The Boosters will again have their Christmas party for the kids on the afternoon of December 8. We're looking at doing a Santa's workshop for the children who'd like to make a present for their parents. That evening we'll be putting on the annual Christmas family dinner. We're adding a white elephant gift exchange this year for all to participate in after the dinner..

September 8 Booster Meeting


The Booster Potluck/Meeting on Saturday, Sept. 8, was jam packed with over 30 members in attendance. There's a real feeling of accomplishment, purpose and camaraderie, especially after the success of Labor Day Weekend. The Boosters are looking into buying special marine Christmas lights to decorate the ferry.
