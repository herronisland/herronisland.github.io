HMC

Island Manager Report

Board Meeting

October 13, 2001

 

Summary

 

Transportation

  • Additional high-density plastic material has been installed on the north inner dolphin on the mainland side.  It will protect the dolphin better than was previously the case, especially during high south wind conditions.
  • Some new expanded metal has been ordered for the ferry ramps.  It will replace portions that have become damaged in years of use.
  • Ferry crew has been provided with a new, easier to use reporting form for use in event of minor damage collisions involving vehicles on the ferry deck or ramps.
  • For Board discussion:  reports on two minor damage incidents that occurred in August while loading/unloading the ferry.

 

Water

  • Wiring and controls upgrades are complete and an “as-built” wiring diagram furnished by the contractor for use as needed in the future.

 

Parks

·        Small boat dock (floats) will be taken out of the water during the last weekend in October.

 

Administration

  • A reminder:  ferry use may be charged for only by service and delivery persons.
  • For Board discussion:  end the island burn ban?

 

Land Use

  • HMC purchase of the County-owned, former Lewis property:  County advises that we should have the final ownership documents within a couple of weeks.
  • For Board discussion:  the boat, boat trailer and travel trailer on the above property.

 

Legal Liaison

·        I. M. worked with attorney during September on employment documents.
