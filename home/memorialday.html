There will be three water events during Memorial Day Weekend:

  SAILBOAT - Two classes: Monohull & Multihull - Saturday 3 PM

  BLINDMAN DINGHY RACE - Sunday 1 PM

  KAYAK - Two classes: 12'6" and over/under - Sunday 3 PM

If the weather is not favorable on Saturday for the sailboat race it will be rescheduled Sunday at 3 PM. Trophies will be presented at the North Beach after each event. Last Labor Day winners will be defending their trophies. It is time to check your boat rigging and wax your boat hulls! Come join the fun as a participant or spectator!
