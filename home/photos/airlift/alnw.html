Airlift Northwest Demonstration
June 2nd, 2001

 The Airlift Northwest chopper coming in for a landing.

More than thirty islanders came to learn more about Airlift.

Flight Nurses Barb & Brian taught the class along with Pilot Ken.

We spent about an hour learning how to help set up the Landing Zone.

After the class, we all went outside for a tour of the helicopter.

The kids had a great time! Julia tried on a flight helmet for size.

Kids and adults got Airlift Northwest pins as souvenirs.



Many thanks to the folks from Airlift Northwest:
 Ken Huston, Pilot
Barb Dempsey, Pediatric Flight Nurse
Brian Currie, Adult Flight Nurse
