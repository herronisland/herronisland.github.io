10/2/13 8:50 pm PDT Update:  The photos from yesterday are displayed below. They show the two engines mounted and nearly ready to go. Today.... we completed the connections and were able to test run our new engines.... the first video is engine 1 and the second video is engine 2. With these successful test runs we anticipate doing sea trials tomorrow (Thursday) and when all is deemed satisfactory we will work to secure and make permanent the necessary "stuff" and hope to return to the island, Friday afternoon.

Captain John
