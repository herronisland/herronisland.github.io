Privacy Statement

 

 

Statement of Privacy

 

Herron Maintenance Co. and www.herronisland.org are committed to maintaining and protecting your privacy.  Our Privacy Policy is detailed below.    If you have comments or questions regarding our policies, please email the Webmaster or the Office.

 

This Privacy Policy applies only to the website, www.herronisland.org, and not to Herron Maintenance Co. business records and office procedures.  The HMC office maintains numerous records required by the HMC By-Laws, and state and federal law.

 

Use of Cookies

 

We believe that cookies belong in the oven, not on your computer.  We don’t ask for them and you do not have to have cookies enabled in your web browser preferences to navigate any portion of our site. 

 

At various places on our site, there are links to outside websites.  We do not know if these outside sites ask for or require cookies.

 

Collecting your personal information

 

We maintain lists of HMC members who have signed up to use the Members Only Area.  This information is limited to the name of the member(s), the preferred email address, the User ID and the Password.

 

We also maintain lists of members who have requested to be placed on our Email News Announcements list or who have agreed to waive their paper copy of the Beachcomber.

 

We may print or retain electronic copies of emails sent to the site, but do not always do so.

 

The web hosting service used by HMC collects and provides to us an access log.  This access log shows which Internet Service Providers have accessed the site during the previous month.  For instance, the log shows that unknown members of America Online visited a specific page a specific number of times in the past month.  The log does not identify which users of that service were doing the viewing or even how many members viewed the page - the information could mean that one AOL member looked at a page ten times, or that ten AOL users viewed the page once each.  We do not retain these logs.

 

Security of Your Personal Information

 

The information we maintain about you (other than User ID’s and Passwords necessary for the operation of the site) is stored on computers other than those of the commercial web hosting service which we use.  This means that in the unlikely event that our site security is violated, the perpetrator would have no access to your personal information because it is not stored on those computers.

 

Use of Your Personal Information

 

We do not sell, rent, lease, give or in any way provide the information we collect to any outside company, organization or person.

 

Information such as email addresses for members may be shared between the HMC Office and the Webmaster.  The Office regularly receives a copy of the list of people who have agreed to waive paper copies of the Beachcomber.  Emails regarding the website which are sent to the Office may be shared with the Webmaster and/or Board Members.  Emails sent to the Webmaster may be shared with the HMC Office and/or Board Members if relevant. 

 

Access to Your Personal Information

 

At any time, you may request a copy of the information (name, email address, User ID, Password, email lists) that the website maintains about you.  If you report any errors in this information, they will be promptly corrected.

 

You may unsubscribe from any list or service by emailing the Office or Webmaster and stating which list or service you wish to discontinue.

 

Protection of Children’s Personal Information

 

We do not collect any information from children.

 

If, in the future, it becomes necessary to collect any information from or about members' children, we will not do so without first receiving permission from the child's parent or guardian.  Any information collected in this manner will receive the same protections as adult information.

 

Site Content

 

The Board of Trustees and the volunteers who run the site have made a commitment that no content will be placed on the website which could not appear in a “G” rated movie.  We are registered with the International Content Rating Association, the ICRA, and our site is appropriate for all ages.

 

Changes to this Statement

 

This Privacy Policy may be changed at any time.  However, if any changes are made to the policy, a prominent notice will appear on the front page of the website explaining the changes and detailing the new policy.  If you are registered for the Members Only Area of the site, you will also receive an email regarding any changes.
