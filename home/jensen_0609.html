On May 12th a maple tree snapped in half and knocked down another tree and a power pole on East Herron Blvd. The power lines and transformer all came down and we had no power for 15 hours. Pen Light did a good repair job. The transformer leaked its fluid onto the ground, damaging the asphalt. No homes were hit, but a major mess was cleaned up by island personnel. Thanks to Dick Mowry for clearing the downed trees so that the power company could replace the pole and transformer. Click here for pictures of the damage from Ted and Stephanie Jensen.
