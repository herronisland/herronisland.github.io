    The Herron Island BOOSTERS is a nonprofit community organization. We include members of Herron Island and all Herron Island members are encouraged to join. We foster community spirit, family, and goodwill throughout the community. We strive to keep the best interests of the Herron Island community at the forefront in our decision-making process.

    Boosters Store
    The Boosters sell Herron Island-themed clothing at their Memorial Day, Fourth of July, and Labor Day events, along with other items from time to time such as key chains, license plate holders, Herron Island stickers, mugs, bags, aprons, and so on.

    We often also sell island directories and calendars, and are sometimes able to mail these. Contact the Boosters President for more information about these.
