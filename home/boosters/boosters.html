    The Herron Island BOOSTERS is a nonprofit community organization. We include members of Herron Island and all Herron Island members are encouraged to join. We foster community spirit, family, and goodwill throughout the community. We strive to keep the best interests of the Herron Island community at the forefront in our decision-making process.
