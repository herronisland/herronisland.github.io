VIRUS ALERT

 The worm KLEZ.H propagates by distributing itself to all entries on an affected computer's address book while disabling application programs and hardware. Although the HMC computers had antivirus software installed, it had not been updated recently enough to recognize the worm. It is strongly recommended that you have antivirus software resident on your computer and update it weekly. If you receive an email where the listed sender is familiar to you, but the subject is a strange phrase such as "welcome to my hometown", "so cool a flash, enjoy it", "let's be friends", or many others, delete the email without opening and contact the person listed as sender. For details on the KLEZ worm, see www.antivirus.com and support.microsoft.com.

The worm also can resist installation of new antivirus software on an infected machine. If you suspect that your computer may be affected and are experiencing difficulty in solving the problem, please email The Webmaster.
